My Bucket List of 2022

Location: WTC KL

Kuala Lumpur International Book Fair is a major event in the global publishing calendar each year. This is so because it is a perfect and strategic place for book lovers to get a variety of books whether old or new publications. In addition, various activities are carried out by publishers throughout the region, thus boosting the celebration and excitement of PBAKL. Book launches, seminars, conventions, and so on are among the activities carried out to meet the needs of the community of readers who belong to all walks of life. Additionally, I managed to make time to participate in this major event although there is several barrier such as packed schedule and other committment. PBAKL 2022 has definitely became one of my sweetest experience and memory of the year!
