My Personal Interests






1) COOKING & BAKING

I enjoy cooking and baking since I was 5 years old. The moment my mother realized that I was into these, she began buying me some baking ingredients and often guide me to conduct these activities during weekend. It was a great experience and I have learnt a lot until now. To add, I even sell some of my cooking at school and around my neighbourhood. I will continue selling once I am finally home again soon!




2) GARDENING

Gardening is much more than a hobby or fun pastime. In fact, there are many proven benefits of gardening that people enjoy. As for me, I have spent a lot of time and money in my effort to build my mini garden at home. Although there is still a lot of challenges such as dying plants and pest attack, I am eager to take care of them despite of having to focus more in nurturing them. Studies show that gardening forces us to keep track of several tasks at the same time.





3) READING

Reading is common among teenagers nowadays. But I have started to have passion in reading since in primary school through the implementation of NILAM program. As for records, I managed to read 378 books and any other reading materials in a year thus gaining triple times NILAM recognition in my primary school years. Until these days, I still read and buy books as my personal collection for mini library at home.
