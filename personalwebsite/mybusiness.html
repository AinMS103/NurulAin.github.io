MY BUSINESS

“Business opportunities are like buses, there’s always another one coming.” – Richard Branson






As a student, I need to figure out a way to earn more pocket money for my own savings in the future instead of depending on PTPTN Loan only.
Therefore, I started to start my own small business from scratch.
Joyful d'Light was chosen as the name of my online gift shop and it takes a lot of time and work to establish this business.
But I feel happy and proud of my achievements for being able to find a way to gain money.
Most importantly, this business helps a lot in various aspects especially in enhancing my communication skills. Besides creating Instagram account as promotional and advertising tools, I decided to create a Telegram channel as my business catalogue.
